# Inoculate — Design & Architecture Plan (v0)

> Pre-implementation blueprint. Code lives in later phases. This doc is the single source of truth for design intent until v0.1 ships.

---

## 0. TL;DR

- **Stack:** Svelte 5 (runes mode) + Vite + TS, single SPA, GH Pages.
- **Genre:** Incremental/idle with a Plague-Inc-style parallel-platform map.
- **Hook:** Six DEPICT technique trees → grow a disinfo empire → AI Saturation era → Mebro reveal turns the game inside out.
- **Constraint pile-up:** mobile-first, < 100 KB initial JS, no backend, no UI kit, no chart lib, no SvelteKit.
- **MVP slice:** Phases 1–3, 2 platforms, 3 DEPICT trees, no Mebro yet. Mebro lands v0.2 — that's the climax, it must be polished.
- **Why this differs from Eras:** parallel platform state (not linear), heat/cure dual meters, dual asset classes (humans vs. bots), and a UI-level third-act reveal.

## 2. Resource Model

### 2.1 Primary resources (per-era, one new per phase)

| # | Resource             | Era                 | Generated by                                        | Sinks                            | Cap source            |
|---|----------------------|---------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|------------------------|
| 1 | Attention            | Grassroots          | Sock puppets, blog posts × platform reach           | Engagement conversion            | Outlet count           |
| 2 | Engagement           | Blog                | Attention × content quality × platform amp          | Followers conversion             | CMS upgrade levels     |
| 3 | Followers            | Social              | Engagement × bot amp × DEPICT bonuses               | Credibility conversion           | Network capacity       |
| 4 | Credibility          | Influencer          | Followers × human-asset prestige tier               | Narrative Dominance conversion   | Reputation laundering  |
| 5 | Narrative Dominance  | Cable & Mainstream  | Credibility × cable bookings × cross-platform synch | Synthetic Reality conversion     | Mainstream pickup      |
| 6 | Synthetic Reality    | AI Saturation       | Narrative Dominance × generator stack               | (none — endgame; consumed by Mebro defections) | AI compute   |

### 2.2 Counter-resources (player must manage, not grow)

| Resource | Scope       | Range | Function                                              |
|----------|-------------|-------|-------------------------------------------------------|
| Heat     | Per-platform| 0..1  | Detection pressure. Decays w/ moderation, fires bans  |
| Cure     | Global      | 0..1  | Real-world inoculation progress. Strictly accumulates |

### 2.3 Conversion model (Invariant 6)

Each conversion is a *separate building/upgrade* the player buys, not automatic. Each has a base rate and a max throughput. At each tick:

```ts
// For conversion of R_in → R_out at building b:
const desired   = b.rate * dt * b.level;
const inputAvail = state.resources[b.inputId];
const outputRoom = capOf(b.outputId) - state.resources[b.outputId];
const fraction   = Math.min(1, inputAvail / desired, outputRoom / (desired * b.ratio));
const consumed   = desired * fraction;
const produced   = consumed * b.ratio * b.qualityMultiplier;
state.resources[b.inputId]  -= consumed;
state.resources[b.outputId] += produced;
```

`qualityMultiplier` folds in DEPICT-tree multipliers, platform amp, handler-capacity fraction, and event buffs.

### 2.4 Closed-form cost curves

All upgrade costs: `cost(L) = base · g^L` with `g ∈ [1.10, 1.20]`. Default `g = 1.15` (matches Eras pacing).

All caps: `cap(L) = capBase + capStep · L` (linear) until paradigm-shift project, then `cap(L) = capBase · capGrowth^L` with `capGrowth ≥ g` (Invariant 1, enforced in `sim.ts`).

### 2.5 Cross-era resource chains (concrete)

```
sock puppet  ──► Attention
                    │ conversion: "RSS aggregator"   r=1:1.2  needs+0 handler
                    ▼
                Engagement
                    │ conversion: "Algorithmic amplifier"  r=1:1.5
                    ▼
                Followers
                    │ conversion: "Booking agent"   r=1:1.0  high handler need
                    ▼
                Credibility
                    │ conversion: "Op-ed pipeline" r=1:0.8 (lossy)
                    ▼
                Narrative Dominance
                    │ conversion: "AI content stack" r=1:2.0 (cheap, but burns Heat)
                    ▼
                Synthetic Reality
```

**Lossy conversions** (Credibility → Narrative Dominance) prevent late-game runaway. **High-yield conversions** (AI stack) intentionally generate heat to set up the Mebro reveal.

|------|------------------------|------------|----------------|----------------------------------------|-------------|-------------|------------------------------------|
| 1    | Anonymous Blogger      | Grassroots | 25 Att         | +1 Att, +0.05 Eng                      | 1           | +0.001      | Tier-1 starter                     |
| 2    | Niche Podcaster        | Blog       | 200 Eng        | +5 Eng, +0.5 Cred                      | 3           | +0.002      | Substack synergy                   |
| 3    | Mid-tier Influencer    | Social     | 1.5k Fol       | +30 Fol, +2 Cred                       | 5           | +0.004      | Platform-specific (one TT/IG/YT)   |
| 4    | Cable Contributor      | Influencer | 12k Cred       | +50 Cred, +5 NarDom                    | 8           | +0.008      | Unlocks Cable era                  |
| 5    | "Thought Leader"       | Cable      | 80k NarDom     | +300 NarDom, +unlocks synergy combo    | 12          | +0.012      | Bookable across multi platforms    |
| 6    | Defection-Resistant Op | AI Sat.    | 500k NarDom    | +1000 NarDom + reduced defection roll  | 15          | +0.015      | Late-game gate                     |

### 3.2 Bot assets (low credibility, high scale, low per-unit heat but compounds)

| Tier | Asset                | Era unlock | Base cost  | Effect                                                    | Heat / tick     |
|------|----------------------|------------|------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|------------------|
| 1    | Sock Puppet          | Grassroots | 5 Att      | +25% Att on one platform                                  | +0.0005          |
| 2    | Small Botnet         | Blog       | 50 Eng     | +15% Eng on one platform                                  | +0.001/100bots   |
| 3    | Astroturf Farm       | Social     | 500 Fol    | +10% Fol globally, but ×1.5 heat/tick on used platforms   | +0.002           |
| 4    | Coordinated Network  | Influencer | 5k Cred    | +20% conversion rate Followers→Cred, ×2 heat              | +0.004           |
| 5    | LLM Content Mill     | Cable      | 30k NarDom | +30% NarDom, generates Synthetic Reality precursor        | +0.008           |
| 6    | Deepfake Generator   | AI Sat.    | 200k NarDom| +50% Synthetic Reality, but +0.02 cure/tick exposure      | +0.020           |

**Asset-class invariant:** *Bot-only* runs cap at Phase 3 (no Credibility produced). *Human-only* runs are 3× slower. Sweet spot ≈ 60/40 humans/bots by ops count in mid-game, flips to 30/70 in AI Sat.

### 3.3 Handler / operator capacity (Invariant 5)

`available_handler_hours = baseHandlers + sum(handlerUpgrade.levels) × 2`
`demanded = sum(asset.count × asset.handlerHrs)`
`labor_fraction = min(1, available/demanded)`

`labor_fraction` multiplies *all* asset production. Below 0.5, fires a "burnout" log line. Hire-handler is its own purchase line — gates pacing.

## 4. DEPICT Trees & Synergy Matrix

Each tree: 8 nodes. Tree shape is a small DAG (not linear) — early branches diverge then meet. Costs in the tree's primary resource (matches the era where the tree blossoms — see below).

### 4.1 Per-tree sketch (8 upgrades each)

| Tree              | Primary resource    | Style                           | Notable nodes                                                   |
|-------------------|---------------------|---------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **D**iscrediting  | Credibility         | Defensive, reduces cure rate    | "Slander template", "Counter-fact-checker", "Whataboutism kit"   |
| **E**motional     | Engagement          | Hi-output, low-credibility      | "Outrage headlines", "Tear-jerker angle", "Fear-cycle composer"  |
| **P**olarization  | Followers           | Multiplier on cross-platform    | "Wedge issue generator", "Us-vs-them framing", "Mob targeter"    |
| **I**mpersonation | Credibility         | Risk/reward — cheap Cred + heat | "Fake expert generator", "Stolen photo bank", "Deepfake atelier" |
| **C**onspiracy    | Engagement / NarDom | Long-tail compounding           | "Coded language", "Q-style drip", "Crisis-actor module"          |
| **T**rolling      | Attention / Heat    | Flat heat ceiling buff          | "Bait detector", "Pile-on coordinator", "Mass-report squad"      |

### 4.2 Synergy matrix (the 8 hand-picked combos out of C(6,2)=15)

| Pair  | Combo name           | Effect                                                              |
|-------|----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------|
| E + P | Wedge Content        | +50% Followers from any post tagged emotional+polarization          |
| I + C | Fake Whistleblower   | Cheap Credibility, fires "exposé" log lines; -10% Heat on Cred ops  |
| D + T | Flood the Zone       | Heat decay rate ×1.4 on platforms where you have both maxed         |
| P + T | Mob Surge            | Trolling targets get +200% reach for 60s after a polarization post  |
| E + C | Moral Panic          | +30% Engagement when Conspiracy upgrade ≥ tier 4                    |
| C + I | False Document Leak  | Unlocks a one-shot project that yanks +5000 NarDom but spikes Cure  |
| D + I | Reverse-Smear        | Discrediting hits also nudge competing Cures back by 1%             |
| P + I | Tribal Trojan        | Polarization platforms gain a fake "leader of your team" asset slot |

Synergies activate when *both* trees pass a threshold (tier 3 each). Each combo is a separate UI card with its own log narration.

### 4.3 Tree unlock predicates

```ts
// Example: Discrediting tree unlocks at Blog era
visible: (s) => s.phase >= 'blog' && s.resources.engagement >= 50
```

Use `visible` predicates per-node so the tree fills in progressively (the §6f "gradual reveal" pattern).

| Platform   | Audience tags                          | Moderation | DEPICT amp (D, E, P, I, C, T)         |
|------------|----------------------------------------|------------|----------------------------------------|
| Facebook   | older, mixed-ideology, family-network  | 0.5        | 1.0, 1.4, 1.5, 1.2, 1.3, 0.9          |
| X (Twitter)| younger-news-junkie, polarized          | 0.3        | 1.3, 1.3, 1.6, 1.1, 1.2, 1.7          |
| TikTok     | youngest, low-context, algorithmic      | 0.6        | 0.8, 1.7, 1.2, 1.4, 1.0, 1.5          |
| YouTube    | broad, long-form tolerant               | 0.7        | 1.5, 1.1, 1.0, 1.3, 1.6, 0.8          |
| Telegram   | self-selected, low-moderation          | 0.1        | 1.2, 1.2, 1.4, 1.5, 1.8, 1.3          |
| Substack   | educated, long-read, paying audience    | 0.2        | 1.6, 0.9, 1.0, 1.7, 1.6, 0.6          |
| Podcast    | trust-laden audio, low fact-check rate  | 0.3        | 1.4, 1.0, 1.2, 1.5, 1.5, 0.7          |

(MVP: Facebook + X. Both familiar, complementary amp profiles, contrasting moderation.)

### 5.2 Heat → ban mechanic per platform

```ts
// Each platform tick:
heatDelta = (sum of asset heat on platform) - (decayRate × moderationStrictness × dt)
heat = clamp(heat + heatDelta, 0, 1)

// Ban triggers:
if (heat > 0.85) probabilityPerTick = (heat - 0.85) / 15
  // → some assets on platform get burned (deleted),
  //    platform.burned = true for 90s lockout
```

`burned = true` zeroes platform.reach until cooldown. Players must diversify platforms — single-platform play is punished.

### 5.3 Cross-platform laundering

A category of upgrades ("Cross-post bot", "Headline launderer") *reduces heat* by a flat amount per tick when an asset's content is rebroadcast through ≥2 other platforms. This is the explicit *content-washing* mechanic from real disinfo research.

## 6. Heat / Cure Mechanics

### 6.1 Heat (local, per-platform)

- Range: `[0, 1]`.
- Sources: per-asset heat × labor_fraction × DEPICT-risk multiplier.
- Decay: `decayRate = 0.005 × moderationStrictness × (1 + laundering bonus)` per tick.
- Visualization: thermometer bar per platform card. Color: green < 0.3 < yellow < 0.6 < orange < 0.85 < red.

### 6.2 Cure (global)

- Range: `[0, 1]`. Strictly monotonically increasing in MVP (resets only on prestige).
- Accumulation:

```ts
cureRate = baseRate
         + 0.0002 × sum(platform.heat)        // visibility breeds resistance
         + 0.0005 × bool(any synergy active)  // combos draw counter-attention
         + 0.001  × (assets.deepfakeGen > 0 ? 1 : 0)  // late-game amp
         - 0.0001 × discreditingLevel         // soft suppression
```

- Thresholds:
  - **0.20** — first fact-checker counter-event window opens (random events become harsher).
  - **0.40** — platform "trust & safety" upgrades go live for the AI; new heat-decay rate is halved.
  - **0.60** — Cable era unlocks **only if** the player has Discrediting tier 5+ (else stuck).
  - **0.80** — **Mebro reveal triggers** (see §8).

### 6.3 Why Cure is monotonic in MVP

Players who can grind cure back down disengage from the inoculation message. The whole point is *the counter is winning while you didn't notice*. Discrediting only slows; never reverses.

## 7. Phase Progression Gates

| From → To                    | Gate                                                                 |
|------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| (start) → Grassroots          | Auto                                                                 |
| Grassroots → Blog             | Attention ≥ 500 AND ≥ 1 paradigm project ("Editorial calendar")      |
| Blog → Social                 | Engagement ≥ 5000 AND own ≥ 1 Niche Podcaster                        |
| Social → Influencer           | Followers ≥ 100k AND any DEPICT tree at tier 4                       |
| Influencer → Cable & Mainstream| Credibility ≥ 1M AND Cure ≥ 0.4 AND Discrediting tier 5             |
| Cable → AI Saturation         | Narrative Dominance ≥ 10M AND any synergy combo active              |
| AI Saturation → Reveal (Mebro)| Cure ≥ 0.80 (auto) OR player triggers "release the deepfake" project |

Transitions are **non-destructive** — every prior era's economy keeps running. New era unlocks new resource + new conversions, doesn't lock old.

Prestige is *separate* from phase progression. Prestige is voluntary post–AI Saturation; resets the run, keeps Legacy.

## 8. Mebro Third-Act Mechanic

**Trigger:** Cure ≥ 0.80 OR project "Release the Deepfake."

### 8.1 Numbers that shift

| Metric                  | Before reveal       | After reveal                                  |
|-------------------------|---------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| Narrative Dominance decay| 0                   | -0.5% per tick (compounds)                    |
| Asset defection roll    | 0                   | 0.5% chance/tick per asset, scaling with cure |
| Heat ceiling (ban)      | 0.85                | 0.50 (everything is fragile)                  |
| New upgrade purchases   | available           | **locked**                                    |
| Conversion rates        | normal              | -20% global (Mebro annotations reduce reach)  |

### 8.2 UI changes (this is mechanical, not narrative)

1. **Mebro fact-check annotations** literally render on existing content cards (an animated badge overlays the per-platform reach number, then strikes it through with a lower number).
2. **Heat bar** transforms into a **Trust** bar — same component, inverse fill, new color ramp. Implementation: pass a `mode: 'heat' | 'trust'` prop; the component handles both.
3. **Defections feed** appears below the log. Live-prepend named assets defecting with their tier and platform. Click to see a debrief card on that asset.
4. **Theme pivot.** `.phase-ai-saturation.reveal` class flips palette to a sterile/clinical blue-white, transitions fonts to slightly off-kilter (small letter-spacing jitter, opt-out via reduce-motion).
5. **Locked tree visuals.** DEPICT trees gain a "FOSSILIZED" overlay; clicking shows the technique's *real-world counter* text.
6. **Topbar Mebro logo** fades in over 10s; tooltip explains it's a real product at mebro.app.

## 10. Svelte 5 Store Architecture (runes, NOT $:)

> Recommendation: use **runes mode** end-to-end. In 2026 runes are the supported path; the legacy `writable` + `$:` pattern works but is being de-emphasized. Runes give us per-property fine-grained reactivity, which matters when `$state.game.upgrades[id]` updates and we don't want the whole platform grid to invalidate.

### 10.1 State containers

```ts
// game/state.svelte.ts
export const game = $state<GameState>(initial());

// game/meta.svelte.ts (persisted separately)
export const legacy = $state<LegacyState>(loadLegacy());
export const achv   = $state<AchvState>(loadAchv());
```

### 10.2 Derived values (read-only, lazily evaluated)

```ts
// game/derived.svelte.ts
export const rates       = $derived(computeRates(game));
export const caps        = $derived(computeCaps(game));
export const etas        = $derived(computeETAs(game, rates, caps));
export const affordable  = $derived(computeAffordable(game, currentBulkMode));
export const heatByPlt   = $derived(game.platforms);  // alias, sugar
export const synergiesOn = $derived(computeSynergies(game.upgrades));
```

### 10.3 Dependency graph

```
game ───┬──► rates ──────┬──► etas
        │                │
        ├──► caps  ──────┘
        │
        ├──► affordable (also depends on bulkMode UI state)
        │
        └──► synergiesOn ──► appliedMultipliers (folds back into rates next tick)
```

The "fold-back" is resolved by the tick loop, not by Svelte: tick reads `synergiesOn` and writes new multipliers into `game` for the next tick. Keeps the reactive graph acyclic.

### 10.4 Persistence boundary

Saves write a snapshot of `game` (not derived). Loading rehydrates `game` via assignment, which retriggers everything downstream. `legacy` and `achv` save on change (debounced 1s).

## 12. Open Design Questions

These are calls I want to make with you before Phase 0.

1. **Runes vs. legacy stores.** I'm recommending runes mode (`$state`/`$derived` end-to-end). Eras shipped on the older pattern; if you'd rather mirror it 1:1 for muscle memory, say so. Tradeoff: runes are cleaner and slightly faster but the sim script and any non-Svelte code (`scripts/sim.ts`) can't import `*.svelte.ts` modules without a build step — meaning game logic must live in plain `.ts` files with stores as thin wrappers. **Recommended:** runes, plain-TS logic core, stores as thin reactive shells.

2. **Branding.** "Inoculate" is fine but generic. Alternates: **Playbook**, **The Operation**, **Engagement Farm**, **Useful Idiots** (provocative — possibly too on-the-nose), **Patient Zero**, **Vector** (best fit IMO — short, virology metaphor, no political coding). My pick: **Vector**.

3. **MVP DEPICT trio.** I chose E/P/T because they're the most kinesthetic and decouple cleanly from cure mechanics. Alternative: pick D/E/I so the *defensive* tree is in MVP and Discrediting balance can be validated early. Default is E/P/T unless you want to surface the heat/cure interplay sooner.

4. **Cure visibility in MVP.** Should v0.1 hide the cure bar entirely (saving the surprise for v0.2 reveal), or show it ticking but with no consequences yet (so v0.1 testers feel the texture of pressure)? **Recommended:** show it, capped at 0.4, no reveal triggers — primes players for v0.2.

5. **Political-balance enforcement.** Should the content-tag balancer be runtime (audit before each headline render) or compile-time (test that the static pool is balanced and trust drawing without replacement)? **Recommended:** compile-time test + runtime random-draw — simpler, easier to debug a partisan run.

6. **Prestige scope in MVP.** Eras shipped prestige early. For Inoculate, prestige in v0.1 with only Phase 1–3 feels thin (no "operation burned down" narrative beat yet). Options: (a) ship Legacy with one node and an in-fiction "rebrand" flavor; (b) defer Legacy entirely to v0.2 when the Mebro burn provides natural prestige justification. **Recommended:** (a) — Legacy is the kind of system whose math you want to validate against the sim *early*, before it has to carry narrative weight.

7. **Mobile bulk-buy UX.** ×1/×10/×100/Max as a 4-segment toggle eats horizontal space. Alternative: long-press an upgrade card to invoke Max. **Recommended:** segmented toggle in topbar (sticky), affects *all* cards globally. Long-press as a discoverable shortcut.

8. **Time-to-victory target.** Eras settled around 4–8 hours of active play to peak. Inoculate's inoculation effect is documented at ~15 min in the Cambridge research. Two answers exist: (a) target ~30–45 min for MVP first reveal (matches academic precedent); (b) target Eras pacing (4–8h) and accept the inoculation effect dilutes for non-completers. **Recommended:** (a). Aggressive pacing. v0.2 ships the reveal at the 45-min mark. The idle layer continues to be playable for hours after, but the *message lands fast*.

9. **Headless sim victory condition.** What does `sim.ts` consider "victory" for MVP — entering Social era? Reaching Followers = 100k? Triggering first prestige? **Recommended:** first prestige (proves the full loop closes).

10. **Tab vs. router for the eventual reveal.** When Mebro fires, do we navigate to a `/debrief` route (clean break, browser back button is meaningful) or animate the existing screen (mechanically intimate, the player feels watched)? **Recommended:** animate in place. The "the game itself transforms around you" feeling is the entire payload.
